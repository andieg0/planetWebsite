NEPTUNE

Name Origin: Named after the Roman god of the sea.
Size: 30,775 miles (49,528 kilometers)
Type: Ice Giant
Mean Temperature: -330°F (-200°C)
Distance from the Sun: 2.8 billion miles (4.5 billion kilometers).
Orbit Duration: 165 Earth Years
Day Duration:: 16 hours
Speed: 3.4 miles (5.4 kilometers) per second.
Other Facts:
  • Neptune is the first planet located through mathematical calculations.
  • It is the only planet in our solar system not visible to the unaided human eye.
  • It is the furthest planet in the Solar System.
  • It has 14 known moons.
  • Neptune has at least five main rings and four prominent ring arcs.
  • Neptune is the Solar System's windiest world. The wind speed can reach more than 1,200 miles per hour (2,000 kilometers per hour).
  • Neptune is the densest one of the giant planets.
