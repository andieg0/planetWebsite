SATURN

Name Origin: Named after the Roman god of agriculture and wealth - Jupiter's father.
Size: 74,897 miles (120,536 kilometers)
Type: Gas Giant
Mean Temperature: - 220°F (-140°C)
Distance from the Sun: 886 million miles (1.4 billion kilometers).
Orbit Duration: 29.4 Earth years
Day Duration:: 10.7 hours
Speed: 6.0 miles (9.7 kilometers) per second.
Other Facts:
  • Saturn is the furthest planet in the Solar System that can be seen by the unaided human eye from Earth.
  • As of June 8, 2023, Saturn has 146 officially confirmed moons in its orbit.
  • It has spectacular ring system where each ring orbits at a different speed around the planet..
  • Saturn's north pole has an interesting atmospheric feature – a six-sided jet stream.
  • It is the only planet in the Solar System with an average density that is less than water.
