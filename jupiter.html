JUPITER

Name Origin: Named after the King of the ancient Roman gods
Size: 88,846 miles (142,984 kilometers)
Type: Gas Giant
Mean Temperature: - 166°F (-110°C)
Distance from the Sun: 484 million miles (778 million kilometers)
Orbit Duration: 11.86 Earth years
Day Duration:: 9.93 hours
Speed: 8.1 miles (13.1 kilometers) per second.
Other Facts:
  • Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System.
  • It has the shortest day in the Solar System.
  • Its enormous magnetic field is 16 to 54 times more powerful than that of the Earth.
  • It has the largest ocean in the Solar System which is made of hydrogen instead of water.
  • It has 95 officially recognized moons and faint ring system.
